================================================================================
TO-BE PROCESS: Enhanced Digital Account Opening (Automated KYC)
================================================================================

PROCESS OVERVIEW:
The enhanced digital account opening process replaces manual KYC checks
with automated identity verification and real-time screening. The goal is
to reduce account opening time to under 5 minutes for low-risk customers
while maintaining regulatory compliance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS:

Step 1: Customer downloads the mobile app or visits the website.

Step 2: Identity verification via Onfido API.
        - Customer uploads a photo of their passport or driving licence
        - Customer takes a selfie
        - Onfido checks the document authenticity and matches the selfie
          to the document photo

Step 3: Real-time KYC check via screening API.
        - PEP screening (politically exposed persons)
        - Sanctions screening (OFSI, UN, EU consolidated lists)
        - Adverse media screening

Step 4: Automated credit reference check via Experian API.
        - Credit score retrieved in real-time

Step 5: Risk scoring algorithm processes all inputs and assigns a
        risk level:
        - Risk factors: PEP/sanctions screening result, credit score,
          Onfido confidence score
        - Output: Low / Medium / High

Step 6: Processing based on risk level:
        - LOW RISK: Instant approval (< 5 minutes)
          → Account created automatically in core banking system
          → Virtual debit card issued immediately
          → Physical card dispatched within 2 working days

        - MEDIUM RISK: Semi-automated review (< 4 hours)
          → Application routed to onboarding team for review
          → Onboarding team reviews screening results and makes a decision
          → If approved, account created and card issued

        - HIGH RISK: Manual compliance review
          → Application routed to compliance team
          → Compliance officer reviews and makes a decision
          → If approved, account created with enhanced monitoring flag

Step 7: Account creation in core banking system (automated for low-risk,
        manual trigger for medium/high-risk).

Step 8: Virtual debit card issued immediately (for low-risk approvals).

Step 9: Physical card issued within 2 working days.

Step 10: Onboarding analytics dashboard for management — shows daily
         application volumes, approval rates, drop-off points, risk
         distribution.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATIONS:
- Application received: instant confirmation email/SMS
- Verification in progress: status update on app
- Decision (approved/rejected): notification with reason
- Account opened: welcome email with account details
- Card dispatched: notification with tracking

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ACCESSIBILITY:
- Mobile app meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- Web fallback available for customers without smartphones
- Branch fallback available for customers who cannot complete digital flow

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DATA RETENTION:
- CDD records retained for 5 years post-relationship (MLR 2017 Reg 40)
- Transaction records retained for 5 years
- Marketing data retained for 6 months post-relationship
- Audit trail: all KYC decisions logged with timestamp, input data,
  and decision rationale

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

INTEGRATIONS:
- Onfido API (identity verification)
- Screening API (PEP/sanctions/adverse media — provider TBD)
- Experian API (credit reference)
- Salesforce CRM (customer record)
- Core banking system (account creation)
- Card issuance system (virtual and physical cards)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SCOPE EXCLUSIONS:
- Ongoing transaction monitoring (handled by existing compliance system)
- Post-onboarding risk rating refresh
- Periodic CDD re-verification
- Non-UK resident onboarding (Phase 2)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EXPECTED BENEFITS:
- Time: < 5 minutes for low-risk customers (from 5-7 days)
- Drop-off rate: reduce from 40% to < 15%
- FTE reduction: from 3 to 1 (monitoring only)
- 100% audit trail via automated logging
- Consistent decision-making via standardised risk scoring
- Real-time customer status updates
